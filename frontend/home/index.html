Tips to build your resume

Contact Information:

Include your full name, phone number, email address, and location (city and state). Make sure your contact information is up-to-date and professional.

Resume Objective or Summary (Optional):

Write a brief, compelling statement that summarizes your career goals and highlights your key qualifications. This is optional but can be beneficial if you want to provide a quick overview of your skills and objectives.

Professional Experience:

Use a clear and bold heading for this section. List your work experience in reverse chronological order (most recent job first). Include the job title, company name, location, and employment dates for each position.

Education:

Create a heading for your education section. Include your highest level of education first, followed by any additional degrees or certifications. Include the name of the institution, degree earned, graduation date, and relevant academic honors or awards.

skills

Use a "Skills" or "Key Skills" heading to showcase your technical, soft, and hard skills relevant to the job you're applying for. List specific skills, such as programming languages, software, foreign languages, or other relevant qualifications.
